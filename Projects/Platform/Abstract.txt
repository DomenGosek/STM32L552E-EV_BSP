The 'Platform' project is a simple CMSIS RTOS2 based template.
Compliant to Cortex Microcontroller Software Interface Standard (CMSIS).

Example functionality:

 - Platform template contains preconfigured drivers (USART, SPI, I2C)

 - Target options:
   - Compiler:                  ARM Compiler optimization Level 1
   - Compiler:Event Recorder:   Enabled
   - CMSIS:RTOS2:Keil RTX5:     Source

 - Preconfigured drivers:
     Peripheral      Pin    STMOD+
   - LPUART1 : TX    PG8    2
               RX    PG7    3
   - SPI3    : NSS   PA4    1
               SCLK  PG9    4
               MOSI  PB5    8
               MISO  PB4    9
   - I2C1    : SCL   PG14   7
               SDA   PG13  10
